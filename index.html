THE STORY OF BANDAR HILIR

AND COMMUNITIES

Melaka, Malaysia















A Local Community is a collection of people who interact and live in the same area. In human communities, intent, belief, resources, preferences, wants, hazards, and a variety of other factors may all be present and shared, influencing people’ identities and degree of cohesion.

This webiste is the local community website, which will introduce to the public about Bandar Hilir and the communities that lived here. The name of our website is the story of Bandar Hilir and Communities and it consist of the elements of city and communities in our logo. The red colour is the symbolic of Bandar Hilir’s buildings that if you visit the Malacca city, it is full of red heritage because of the Dutch history. Not only that, the word of ‘communities’ appeared is because there are many types of communities that lived in Bandar Hilir so it is the “ies” is the symbolic to show that they have various kind of communities. Hence, in this website will be tell the story of bandar hilir that people not know especially out of the Melaka
