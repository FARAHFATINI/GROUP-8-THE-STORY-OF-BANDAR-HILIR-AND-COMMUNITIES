what is the interesting things in medan samudera bandar hilir melaka?

foodcourt

Medan Samudera there is have a food court at level two. There have many shop lot that are sell the variety of food. They also have provide the famous dishes in melaka which is Asam Pedas. Even though, the foodcourt at this place are not good as five star restaurant, but this place is very strategic becuase are located in the town of melaka.

food

in medan samudera, there also have a variety food that can buy at the shop for the souvinier. For example, there have sell dried food such as cencalok, dodol, wajik and many more for souvinier and make a stock at their home. The most popular food at melaka are cencalok, belacan bakar and udang kering. So, they can buy these foods at this place.

facilities

people also can relax at medan samudera because there have facilities that are provided to the people. For example, there are provided public toilets, surau, and car park. So, people can to medan samudera to rest and go to the pray after sightseeing at the town.

souvenier

Besides that, people can buy something form melaka to make a memories. For example, in medan samudera there also sell the keychain, t-shirt, hat, and there also sell the handcarft such as basket, tudung saji and many more.
