Kebudayaan Baba Nyonya 2019

Kebudayaan Baba Nyonya 2019 was held in Zheng He Duo Xuan Art Gallery, Lorong Hang Jebat Melaka by "Peranakan Baba Nyonya" and was officiated by YBhg. Director Genera the department of National Unity and National Integration Malaysia

Jonker Walk Market during MCO

Jonker Walk Night Market re-opened back from MCO (movement control order) in 2021, with the new SOP, that must be follow by the public within the 1m distance.

Chinese New Year Event

Chinese New Year Event 2022 will be held at Jonker Street, Bandar Hilir Melaka, to celebrate the Chinese New Year in 30th January 2022. It is full with the Lion dance of Chinese Cultural and the fellow performances.

Pakatan Harapan wins second seats in Bandar Hiliredssss

Pakatan Harapan wins second seat of Bandar Hilir in Melaka State election on 2021, November 20. Its Candidate Leng Chau Yen has garnered 7,017 votes compared with the 1, 085 votes for BN's Lee Kah Serani.

Bandar hilir Melaka, Quiet without occupants

During MCO, certain area of Malacca especially Bandar Hilir was quiet without the visitors and even the Malacca's people. It is because of the Restriction of movement especially between the states.
